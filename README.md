# FSociety

FSociety is a revolutionary group of hackers that strive to take down corrupt corporations and is focused on cybersecurity awareness and enlightening the public about the importance of protecting their digital privacy.

## Mission
Our mission at FSociety is to promote ethical hacking, educate the public on cybersecurity issues, and empower individuals with the knowledge to protect themselves online.
